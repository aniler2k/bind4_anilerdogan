Hypnotic Black Magic

Label Head/Event Organizer/DJ

Hypnotic Black Magic (Apolline Guedon) is a Berlin-based DJ, event organiser and label owner. Since 2018, they are organising events through Art Bei Ton and Ambient Sleepers. Their aim is to promote diverse international artists within gender-balanced line ups, focusing of offering safe space for flinta* and queer artists to perform.

Tibet Tunaboyu

Sound Engineer/Live Tech

Tibet Tunaboyu is a London-based sound engineer, musician and live music technician. Since 2020 they have been working in various roles in the London live music scene.
